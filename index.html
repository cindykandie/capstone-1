Main Program

  • Lecture

    Movement that promotes the idea of saving and investing aggressively in order to reach a state of financial independence, where one's passive income is sufficient to cover their living expenses, at a young age

  • CC Exhibition

    F.I.R.E movement is to achieve financial independence and the freedom and flexibility it brings, in order to pursue other passions and interests, or simply to enjoy a more leisurely and fulfilling life

  • Forum

    Investing aggressively in order to reach a state of financial independencein order to pursue other passions and interests, or simply to enjoy a more leisurely and fulfilling life

  • Workshop

    The idea behind F.I.R.E. is to have the freedom and flexibility to live one's life on one's own terms, without being financially dependent on a job or traditional sources of income!

  • F.I.R.E Ignite

    Master the ability to retire at an early age, typically in one's 30s or 40s, by saving and investing a high percentage of one's income, living frugally, and building passive income streams.
